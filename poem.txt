A Short Analysis of Maya Angelou’s ‘Still I Rise’
‘Still I Rise’ is a poem by the American poet Maya Angelou (1928-2014), 
published in her 1978 collection And Still I Rise. A kind of protest poem which is defiant as well as celebratory, ‘Still I Rise’ is about the power of the human spirit to overcome discrimination and hardship, with Angelou specifically reflecting her attitudes as a black American woman.
